How to use this dashboard

reads your Star Citizen Game.log — read-only, nothing is sent anywhere

🚀 Quick start

  1. Leave this open while you play — it auto-updates every 2s.
  2. Live feed = what's happening now. Analyze = your history & stats.
  3. Run hauling missions? Open the 🚚 Cargo tab for an optimized delivery route.
  4. Flip 💡 Tips (top-right) for hints under each panel.

📡 Live feed

Mission & combat activity — objectives, contracts and combat lines as they log.

Missions — grouped by contract, outcomes shown (✅ complete / ⤺ abandon / ✖ fail).

Collisions — ship crashes on the current build (who hit what, closing speed).

Type in the filter box to narrow the feed by keyword.

🔎 Analyze

Your activity over time — heatmap, mission outcomes, types, factions, pilot stats.

Every chip/slice cross-filters all panels. Use the period range to scope months.

Spans backloaded history (npm run backfill) plus the live session.

🚚 Cargo route

Active hauling contracts grouped by pickup → dropoff, ordered by moon/planet.

⟳ Route recomputes; auto-refresh updates it as you pick up cargo.

Enter your ship SCU for capacity warnings. ⏳ carried over = seen a previous session; verify it's still active.

💡 Good to know (honesty)

  • Kills aren't logged on current builds (only ≤4.3.0) — the panel stays empty live.
  • Combat is an inferred proxy from mission objectives, not exact kills.
  • Everything is self-reported from your own machine's log.

⭐ Mission & combat activity — objectives, contracts & any combat lines. Hit Copy all to send me new formats.

💀 Kills — kills involving the running player (yours + your deaths). Third-party kills are not logged.

💡 This stays empty on current game builds — CIG stopped logging kills after 4.3.0. It only fills on older (≤4.3.0) logs.
